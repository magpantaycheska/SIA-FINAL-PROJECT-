Company History

The BAP Construction and Development Corporation has more than three prolific decades of experience in the construction industry, founded by Mr. Benjamin A. Perez on March 2, 1993, under BAP Construction as a sole proprietor. Mr. Benjamin A. Perez’s eldest son, Marvin Jay B. Perez, followed in the footsteps of his father as he was introduced to the construction business at an early age. In 2007, Marvin Jay officially joined the company after being licensed as Civil Engineer and Materials Engineer I and took over the construction business’ management in 2014. From our humble beginnings, BAP Construction has expanded to become one of the established major contractors in the construction business.

Our commitment to innovation and excellence led us to our accomplished goals. The company upgraded from Category D to A and then to Category AAA. The company name changed from BAP Construction to BAP Construction and Development Corporation in 2013. The company has continued to see great potential in furthering its expansion by opening the main office in Alabang, Muntinlupa City in 2023 to reach out to more clients.

We are currently a PCAB accredited contractor since 1993, with the PCAB license no. 16215. The SEC-registered contractor with Sec. Registration number CS201222782.

We at BAP Construction and Development Corporation are accustomed to constructing, rehabilitating, and improving roads, bridges, water supply, flood control and drainage systems, multi-purpose buildings, asphalt overlay, and other related vertical and horizontal civil structures.

We pride ourselves on our personal and individual approach to improvements and constructions, which is always taken to achieve high-standard professional projects, dealing with private and government institutions. Acquired through the rich experience and knowledge we have are our successful projects that not only meet the customer’s requirements but are finished to the highest standard.
