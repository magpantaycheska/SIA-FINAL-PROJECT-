Company Overview

BAP Construction and Development Corporation is a trusted construction company providing quality infrastructure, engineering solutions, and project management services for various industries.

Company History

The BAP Construction and Development Corporation has more than three prolific decades of experience in the Construction Industry founded by Mr. Benjamin A. Perez on March 2, 1993, under BAP Con- struction as a sole proprietor. Mr. Benjamin A. Perez’s Eldest Son, Marvin Jay B. Perez followed in the footsteps of his father as he was introduced to the construction business at an early age. In 2007, Marvin Jay Officially joined the company after being licensed as Civil Engineer and Materials Engineer I and took over the construction busi- ness’ management in 2014. From our humble beginnings, BAP Con- struction has expanded to become one of the established major con- tractors in the construction business.

Our commitment to innovation and excellence led us to our ac- complished goals. The company upgraded from Category D to A and then to Category AAA. The company name changed from BAP Con- struction to BAP Construction and Development Corporation in 2013. The company has continued to see great potential in furthering its expansion by opening the main office in Alabang, Muntinlupa City in 2023 to reach out to more clients.

We are currently a PCAB accredited contractor since 1993; with the PCAB license no. 16215. The SEC-registered contractor with Sec. Registration number CS201222782.

We at BAP Construction and Development Corporation are ac- customed to constructing, rehabilitating, and improving roads, bridges, water supply, flood control and drainage systems, multi-purpose build- ings, asphalt overlay, and other related vertical and horizontal civil structures.

We pride ourselves on our personal and individual approach to the improvements and constructions which is always taken to achieve high-standard professional projects, dealing with private and govern- ment institutions. Acquired through the rich experience and knowledge we have are our successful projects that not only meet the customer’s requirements but are finished to the highest standard.

Mission

We are commited to leading the industry in honesty, safety, integrity and exceeding our customer's expectation and to provide an outstanding, cost efficient and reliable service to every project.

Vision

We at BAP Construction and Development Corporation eagerly anticipate taking on future challenges and obstacles in the construction industry. Our aim is to become widely admired as a construction company that consistently adds value and wisdom to all the services we provide to our clients.
